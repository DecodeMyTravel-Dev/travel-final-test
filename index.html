Baggage Considerations: 2026 Manual

1. The Standard for the "Big Three" (American, Delta, United)
Standard has moved to Volumetric Compliance. If your bag doesn't slide into the sizer without a shove, it's a gate-check.

  • Personal Item: 17x10x9in. Must fit under seat. Tip: Basic Economy restricted to this.
  • Carry-On: 22x14x9in. Must include wheels and handles.
  • Checked Bag (Economy): 50 lbs limit. Fees average $45–$50.
  • Checked Bag (First/Business): 70 lbs (32 kg) limit. Most premium tickets include 2-3 bags free.

2. Regional Airlines
Carry-ons often capped at 15–22 lbs (7–10 kg). The Regional Rule: Many allow only 10kg for Economy. Anything over is charged per kilo.

3. Surcharges & The 2026 "Fuel Factor"
Fuel Surcharge (YQ/YR codes) and $10-$25 "Security & Fuel Handling" fees common on regional checked bags.

Airport & Port Exchange Traps

1. Arrivals Trap: Exchange kiosks (Travelex, etc.) at airport arrivals are predatory. Expect to lose **12-15%** of value.

2. Private ATMs: Machines inside terminals or at cruise piers (e.g., Euronet) charge massive fees ($5-$12) and 10% worse conversion rates.

3. Strategy: Walk two blocks away from the port/airport to find a "Mainline" local bank machine for the best rates.
