Baggage Considerations: 2026 Manual

1. The Standard for the "Big Three" (American, Delta, United)
Standard moved to Volumetric Compliance. If it doesn't slide into the sizer without a shove, it's a gate-check.

  • Personal Item: 17x10x9in. Must fit under seat. (Basic Economy restricted to this).
  • Carry-On: 22x14x9in. Must include wheels and handles.
  • Checked Bag (Economy): 50 lbs (23 kg). Fees average $45-$50.
  • Checked Bag (First/Business): 70 lbs (32 kg).

2. Regional Airlines: Caps of 15–22 lbs (7–10 kg). 3. Fuel Factor: Variable Fuel Surcharge (YQ/YR codes) and $10-$25 fees common.

Exchange & Port Strategy

1. Arrivals Trap: Exchange kiosks are predatory. Expect to lose **12-15%** of value.

2. Private ATMs: Machines at cruise piers charge massive fees ($5-$12) and 10% worse conversion rates.

3. Dynamic Conversion: ALWAYS pick Local Currency at terminals to avoid predatory rates.
